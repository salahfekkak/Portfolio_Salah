SKILLS

USER RESEARCH

I work in small groups with my collaborators, conducting interviews to collect quantitative and qualitative data. At that point, I focus on identifying our potential users, creating proto-pesonas, Empathy maps, Storyboards and coming up with different user scenarios.

UI DESIGN

The UI design phase is my favorite phase of the process. Using the Atomic Design method, I carefully choose the Typography and colors that best identify the product. Then, I conduct multiple tests to see if the design is functional and easy to use.

UI_PROTOTYPE

I always make sure to have smooth and clean animations by using Figma, XD, and Invision, or even coding some pages in HTML/CSS/Javascripts. Doing this ensures that the stakeholders will have a clear idea about the final product.

WEBSITE REDESIGN
